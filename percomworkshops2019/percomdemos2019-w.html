Welcome Message

Welcome to the 17th year of the IEEE Pervasive Computing and Communications (PerCom) conference. The objective of the demo session is to provide a venue for practitioners and academics to present the most innovative hands-on technical breakthroughs from their past year of research. We are delighted by the rich variety of demos we have this year and expect they will capture the imagination of PerCom attendees. From 24 submissions we have selected 14 demos for the session. These demos include examples of working prototypes, novel applications, and live experiments. Each demo will also have a corresponding 3-page demo abstract included in the published proceedings.

Finally, we would like to thank the Technical Program Committee and members of the organizing committee and for their tireless support in this year's demo session.
