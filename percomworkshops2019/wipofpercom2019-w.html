PerCom WiP 2019: PerCom Work in Progress on Pervasive Computing and Communications, 2019 - Welcome and Committees

We would like to welcome you to the Work in Progress (WiP) Session of the IEEE International Conference on Pervasive Computing and Communications (PerCom) 2019. The goal of PerCom's WiP Session is to offer a rich and varied set of early-stage pervasive computing contributions from academia and industry. The WiP Program Committee and the WiP Chairs selected a total of 13 papers from a pool of 24 submissions. The key evaluation criteria included the extent and significance of the research contribution, the relevance to PerCom, the potential impact on the field, and the presentation quality. We would like to sincerely thank the WiP authors for contributing to a rich and varied program. Thanks are also due to the WiP Program Committee members for their dedication and diligence in careful review of the submissions. We sincerely hope you that will enjoy and benefit from the PerCom 2019 WiP program.

Takuya Maekawa and Peizhao Hu, Chairs, PerCom WiP 2019
