PerCom PhD Forum 2019: PhD Forum on Pervasive Computing and Communications 2019 - Welcome and Committees

Welcome Message from the Ph.D Forum Chairs

We are pleased to introduce the Ph.D Forum on Pervasive Computing and Communications, 2019. The Ph.D Forum provides an opportunity for Ph.D students to present their ongoing dissertation research, receive valuable feedback from reputed international researchers, and more importantly build mutual relationship among them. The Forum is organized in two phases: during the first phase, the students participate in a half-day long workshop presenting and discussing their research agenda with reputed researchers; during the second phase, they provide a one-minute pitch on their research and then present it at the poster session during the main conference. This year, we got 23 submissions, among which 17 papers are accepted after a careful reviews. We thank all the authors for their participation in the Ph.D Forum.
